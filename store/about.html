About This Store

Stadia App Store is a curated listing of modern, high-quality applications and tools, built with a focus on usability, performance, and open web technologies. Our mission is to elevate productivity and creativity by showcasing purposeful, delightful software – whether you're a developer, designer, hobbyist, or everyday user.

Every app here is selected for polish, utility, and reliability, aiming to meet the practical needs of today's creators. From productivity boosters to fun games and developer utilities, Stadia makes it easy to find and access great web experiences with no install required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I submit my own app?
A: Yes! If you built something awesome and want it considered, email us or open an issue on GitHub.

Q: Are all apps here free?
A: Most are free to use or try. Some may offer upgrades or pro features, always with transparent info before you launch the app.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: We list only apps that respect privacy and do not require unnecessary data. For external apps, refer to each app's own privacy policy.
